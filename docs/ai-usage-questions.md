# AI Usage Survey Questions

Questions for SXSW and AI Future Raffle campaigns to understand how participants use AI.

## Campaign Context

- **SXSW Campaign**: Tech/innovation conference audience, raffle for signed copy (50 winners)
- **AI Future Raffle**: General audience interested in AI transformation, free book raffle

## Suggested Questions

### 1. AI Usage Frequency
**Question**: "How often do you currently use AI tools?"

**Type**: Multiple choice (single select)

**Options**:
- Daily
- Weekly
- Monthly
- Rarely
- Never

**Purpose**: Understand adoption level and engagement patterns

---

### 2. Primary AI Tool
**Question**: "Which AI tool do you use most frequently?"

**Type**: Multiple choice (single select)

**Options**:
- ChatGPT
- Claude
- Gemini
- GitHub Copilot
- Midjourney/DALL-E
- Other (with text field)
- I don't use AI tools yet

**Purpose**: Identify market leaders and tool preferences

---

### 3. AI Use Cases
**Question**: "What do you primarily use AI for?"

**Type**: Multiple choice (multi-select)

**Options**:
- Writing/content creation
- Code/software development
- Research and learning
- Work productivity
- Creative projects (art, music, etc.)
- Personal assistant tasks
- Business strategy/analysis
- I'm just exploring

**Purpose**: Understand practical applications and use case patterns

---

### 4. Biggest AI Challenge
**Question**: "What's your biggest challenge with AI?"

**Type**: Multiple choice (single select)

**Options**:
- Don't know where to start
- Concerns about accuracy/reliability
- Privacy/security worries
- Finding the right tool for my needs
- Learning how to write effective prompts
- Keeping up with new tools
- No significant challenges

**Purpose**: Identify pain points and opportunities for the book to address

---

### 5. SXSW-Specific Question
**Question**: "What brings you to SXSW?"

**Type**: Multiple choice (single select)

**Options**:
- Work/business development
- Learning about AI/tech trends
- Networking
- Startup pitch/demo
- Entertainment/music
- First time exploring

**Purpose**: Understand attendee motivations and segment audience

**Note**: Only add to SXSW campaign

---

### 6. AI Future Needs
**Question**: "What's one area where you'd like AI to help you more?"

**Type**: Free text response

**Options**: N/A (free text field)

**Purpose**: Gather qualitative insights about unmet needs and desires

**Note**: Optional - adds depth but requires more effort from participants

---

## Implementation Priority

**Recommended for both campaigns**:
1. AI Usage Frequency (#1)
2. Primary AI Tool (#2)
3. AI Use Cases (#3)
4. Biggest AI Challenge (#4)

**Campaign-specific add-ons**:
- SXSW only: What brings you to SXSW (#5)
- Optional for both: AI Future Needs (#6)

## Benefits of These Questions

1. **Audience Insights**: Understand AI maturity and adoption levels
2. **Market Research**: Tool preferences and competitive landscape
3. **Pain Points**: Identify what readers struggle with (inform marketing)
4. **Content Ideas**: Use responses for future content/blog posts
5. **Segmentation**: Better targeting for follow-up campaigns
6. **Book Positioning**: Validate that Cognitive Kin addresses real needs

## Technical Notes

- Questions should be added via admin UI at `/admin/campaigns/[id]/edit`
- Set as optional so they don't block raffle entries
- Export responses with addresses for analysis
- Consider A/B testing question order or phrasing
